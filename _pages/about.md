I am a Professor of Simulation of Complex Adaptive Systems at the [University of Amsterdam](https://ivi.uva.nl/) 🇳🇱 where I am a member of the [Computational Science Lab](https://ivi.uva.nl/research/computational-science-lab-csl.html) of the [Informatics Institute](https://ivi.uva.nl/), and Professor of [Informatics](https://luddy.iu.edu/academics/units/informatics.html) and [Cognitive Science](https://cogs.indiana.edu/index.html) at [Indiana University Bloomington](https://luddy.iu.edu/) 🇺🇸.

I served as [Informatics](https://luddy.iu.edu/academics/units/informatics.html) department chair at [Indiana University Bloomington](https://luddy.iu.edu/) where I am presently the director of the Center for Social and Biomedical Complexity. I am a member of the board of directors of the center for [Urban Mental Health](https://ias.uva.nl/research-themes/health-complexity/urban-mental-health/urban-mental-health.html), director of the SOCIAL lab at Indiana University, and a [Synergy Program for Analysing Resilience and Critical transitionS](https://sparcs-center.org/) fellow at the [Wageningen University and Research](https://www.wur.nl/). I was formerly a staff scientist at the [Los Alamos National Laboratory](https://www.lanl.gov/) in New Mexico and an Assistant Professor at the [Department of Computer Science of Old Dominion University](https://www.odu.edu/academics/programs/linked/computer-science) in Virginia. I have a PhD (VUB, 2001) in Psychology from the [Vrije Universiteit Brussel](https://www.vub.be/nl)🇧🇪.

I published more than 100 [articles](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=jDmcdsUAAAAJ) on computational science, computational psychology, AI, network science, complex systems, social media analytics, and informetrics, receiving nearly 25,000 citations (h-index=50, i10=100). My research has been funded by the [NIH](https://www.nih.gov/), [NSF](https://www.nsf.gov/), [DARPA](https://www.darpa.mil/), [IARPA](https://www.iarpa.gov/), [EDA](https://www.eda.gov/), [NASA](https://www.nasa.gov/), and [Andrew W. Mellon Foundation](https://www.mellon.org/). My recent work is focused on aunraveling the complex dynamics of individual and collective mental health, with respect to the affective, cognitive, and behavioral effects of social media on well-being.
